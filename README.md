## What is this?

A public experiment.

Can one person — with only evenings after work, AI, programming, and creativity — earn **₹65,000** before GTA VI launches?

No salary money. No loans. No shortcuts.

Everything happens in public. Every rupee tracked. Every lesson shared.

| Goal | ₹65,000 |
|------|---------|
| Deadline | Before GTA VI launches |
| Starting amount | ₹0 |
| Salary allowed | ❌ Never |

## The Character

A convenience store owner who has worked in Los Santos for years.

Nothing surprises him anymore.

Bank robberies. Explosions. Flying motorcycles. Tanks. Five-star wanted levels.

To him — it's just another workday.

**Personality:** Dry humor. Calm. Sarcastic. Never panics. Treats complete chaos like normal customer service.
